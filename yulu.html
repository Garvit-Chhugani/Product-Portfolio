KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • A smooth onboarding flow isn’t just about signups—it’s about getting users to their first ride with minimal effort.
  • Small friction points, like extra verification steps, can make users drop off before they even start using the service.
  • In mobility, speed is everything—every extra step in the journey feels like a delay in reaching the destination.
