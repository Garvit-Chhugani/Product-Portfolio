Zomato

A product enhancement proposal to redesign the Zomato Driver App, focusing on improving usability, boosting efficiency, and simplifying the delivery experience.

Problem Statement

You are a Product Designer for a food delivery platform (Swiggy/Zomato, etc.). The platform’s management has decided to redesign its driver application to make it easier for its drivers to understand and use. They’ve asked you to propose a high-level user flow and design.

KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • Reinforced the importance of user research in identifying real-world pain points and designing targeted solutions.
  • Showcased how small UX changes can have a significant impact on operational efficiency and retention.
  • Delivery partners prioritize clarity and speed over complex features, making intuitive design a key differentiator.
  • A well-structured onboarding experience can reduce drop-offs and improve engagement from day one.
  • The gig economy thrives on seamless app experiences, where every second saved translates to better earnings and improved service.
  • Delivery logistics rely heavily on real-time decision-making, making clear and actionable UI crucial for efficiency.
