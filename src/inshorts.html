KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • A powerful recommendation engine doesn’t just show content — it understands user behavior to surface what truly matters.
  • Personalization improves engagement, but too much filtering can create an echo chamber, limiting diverse perspectives.
  • Search and discovery should feel effortless — users should find relevant content even when they don’t know exactly what they’re looking for.
