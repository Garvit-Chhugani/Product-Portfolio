LEAPS by Analyttica

At Analyttica, I served as an Associate Product Manager, driving data-driven solutions that empowered analysts and data scientists to enhance data literacy while delivering measurable business impact.

About the Company

Role Context

LEAPS by Analyttica is a B2B SaaS platform tailored for data scientists and analysts to elevate data literacy through interactive, low-code/no-code capabilities. Designed to simplify complex data workflows, it enables engineers to explore data, validate hypotheses, and drive data-driven decisions.

As one of four Associate Product Managers, I collaborated with cross-functional teams of PM, designers, including engineering design, QA, and business teams. Reporting to the Product Head, I served as a strategic partner in strategy setting and decision-making processes. My primary responsibility was managing the product roadmap, while ensuring alignment with user needs and business goals.

Key Features/Projects

Conversational Data Coach | User Onboarding & Personalization Journey | Engagement Dashboard | Enhanced Search & Documentation

Feature/Project Name

Conversational Data Coach

Objective

To improve user query resolution and enhance the platform's usability through more conversational assistance.

Problem Statement

Users struggled with navigating the platform's modules, impacting their productivity and engagement with the platform.

Approach

Integrated OpenAI API to develop a Conversational Data Coach that provides intuitive assistance for data exploration and analysis. Collaborated with design and engineering teams to refine user flows for seamless interaction.

Result/Outcome

Reduced query resolution time by 35%, increasing user satisfaction and engagement.

Feature/Project Name

Personalized Onboarding Journey and Home Page

Objective

To enhance user engagement by delivering tailored experiences based on user roles and behavior.

Problem Statement

New users struggled to navigate the platform, resulting in lower adoption rates and usage.

Approach

Designed a data-driven onboarding experience with personalized content based on user roles. Implemented A/B testing to identify effective user flows and iteratively optimized the onboarding flow.

Result/Outcome

Boosted user engagement by 17% within two months of implementation.

Feature/Project Name

Engagement Dashboard

Objective

To provide users and administrators with actionable insights into platform usage and progress.

Problem Statement

Users lacked visibility into their engagement metrics, making it difficult to track progress or identify areas for improvement.

Approach

Built an Engagement Dashboard with intuitive visualizations and performance tracking capabilities. Incorporated user feedback to refine metrics and usability.

Result/Outcome

Improved user retention by 14% by offering clear and actionable insights.

Feature/Project Name

Enhanced Search and Documentation

Objective

To optimize the platform’s search functionality and improve user support materials.

Problem Statement

Users encountered irrelevant or no search results, while documentation gaps led to increased support requests.

Approach

Developed a new search logic (Eliminate-Search-Filter) to improve accuracy and implemented a comprehensive revamp of the documentation.

Result/Outcome

Reduced irrelevant search results by 24% and support requests by 32%.
