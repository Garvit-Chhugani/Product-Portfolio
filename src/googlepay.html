KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • A well-designed referral program isn’t just about rewards — it’s about creating a seamless invite and onboarding experience.
  • Incentives drive participation, but the real challenge is ensuring referred users stay engaged beyond the initial reward.
  • The best referral programs feel natural, integrating effortlessly into existing user behaviors rather than feeling like a forced promotion.
